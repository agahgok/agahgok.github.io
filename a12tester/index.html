Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

What is Google Play's closed testing requirement?

Google requires new apps to be tested by at least 12 users for 14 consecutive days before you can publish to production. This is part of their effort to improve app quality on the Play Store.

How long does the testing process take?

The testing period is 14 days as required by Google. Our testers will join your closed test within 24-48 hours of your submission, so you can start the countdown quickly.

Are the testers real people?

Yes! All our testers are real users with real Android devices. No bots, no fake accounts. This ensures you meet Google's requirements properly.

What do I need to submit my app?

You just need your app's closed testing link from Google Play Console. You'll also need to provide basic information like your app name and package name.

Can I really get credits for free?

Absolutely! You can watch rewarded video ads to earn 10 credits each. It's a great way to get started without spending any money.

Is my app data secure?

Yes. We only need your closed testing link - we never access your developer console or source code. Your app data is encrypted and stored securely.
